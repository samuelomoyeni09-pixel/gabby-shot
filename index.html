Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session with Gabby Shot?
Simply fill out the booking form on this page or send us a WhatsApp message directly. We'll get back to you quickly to confirm your date, location and package. It's that easy!
Do you travel outside your city for shoots?
Yes! We cover shoots across Nigeria — Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Benin and more. Travel fees may apply depending on the distance. Reach out and we'll work something out.
How long does it take to receive my photos/videos?
Turnaround time depends on your package — typically 3 days for the Starter package, 5 days for Pro, and priority delivery for Elite. For urgent requests, contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you require a deposit before the shoot?
Yes, a deposit is required to secure your booking date. The amount and payment structure will be discussed when you reach out. We accept bank transfer and mobile payments.
Can I get both photos and video for my event?
Absolutely. Our Pro and Elite packages include both photography and video coverage. You can also request a custom package that combines exactly what you need. Just let us know!
What if the weather or something goes wrong on shoot day?
No stress at all — we'll reschedule to a convenient date for you at no extra charge. Things happen and we totally understand. We're flexible and your satisfaction comes first.
